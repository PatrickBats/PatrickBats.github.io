About

I'm an undergraduate at Rice University studying Operations Research and Statistics. I work on problems at the intersection of machine learning theory and generative modeling, with a focus on understanding how models behave when trained on synthetic data.

Research Interests:

  • Synthetic data and model autophagy
  • Generative models (diffusion models, GANs)
  • Vision-language models and cognitive science
  • Spectral analysis and model interpretability

Projects

Multi-Layer Purrceptron

Comparative analysis of a custom 5-layer CNN vs. ResNet50 for fine-grained cat breed classification. Used Grad-CAM to reveal how from-scratch models focus on local features while pre-trained models leverage holistic context.

PyTorch, Deep Learning, Transfer Learning

Learning Through Little Eyes

Benchmark for evaluating attribute discrimination in vision-language models trained on infant egocentric data. Comparing CVCL with CLIP reveals infant-scale learning excels at size discrimination while web-scale training dominates color grounding.

CLIP, Vision-Language Models, Cognitive Science

MAD Detection

Investigating Model Autophagy Disorder in generative models (DDPM, DDIM, GANs, StyleGAN) by analyzing the spectrum of Jacobians in local input spaces across synthetic training generations.

PyTorch, Generative Models, Spectral Analysis
